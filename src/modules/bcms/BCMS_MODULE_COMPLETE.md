## Overview
The BCMS module manages the organizational hierarchy and physical infrastructure of the Proactiv Fitness platform. It provides comprehensive management of countries, regions, business units, locations/centers, rooms/resources, holiday calendars, and academic/business terms.

## Implementation Summary

### 1. Entities Implemented (7)
1. **Country** - Top-level geographical entity
2. **Region** - Sub-divisions within countries
3. **Business Unit** - Organizational units (HQ, franchises, partners)
4. **Location/Center** - Physical fitness centers
5. **Room/Resource** - Rooms and resources within locations
6. **Holiday Calendar** - Holiday management for scheduling
7. **Term** - Academic/business terms
